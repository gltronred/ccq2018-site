Purpose and Scope

Computation and cryptography with qu-bits is workshop on different topics of theory of computation, computational compexity and cryptography.

The topics include, but are not limited to:

  • Quantum and classic computing
  • Algorithms
  • Computational complexity
  • Cryptography
  • Quantum and random walks
  • Automata theory
